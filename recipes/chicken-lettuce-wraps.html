Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Description

These healthy and flavorful wraps will make you never want to order from PF-Changs again! Recipe from theCozyCook.com

Ingredients

Chicken Wraps

  • 1 lb. ground chicken
  • Salt/pepper
  • 1 tbsp. peanut oil
  • 3 cloves fresh garlic, minced
  • 1/3 c. lulienned carrots
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, finely diced
  • 1 tbsp. smooth peanut butter, optional
  • 8 butter lettuce leaves, or romaine or iceberg

Sauce

  • 1/2 c. chicken broth
  • 1/4 c. soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp. honey
  • 1 tbsp. lime juice
  • 1 tsp. sriracha
  • 1/2 tsp. ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp. sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp. cornstarch

Toppings

  • 1/3 c. honey roasted peanuts
  • 3 green onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp. toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Whisk together the sauce ingredients and set aside.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the chicken. Cook for 6 minutes, stirring continuously. Add garlic, carrots, and peppers and cook for 1 more minute, or until the chicken is cooked through. Drain any excess liquid from teh chicken if needed.
  3. Add the sauce and bring to a boil. Stir in the peanut butter, if using. Once thickened, remove from heat.
  4. Spoon the filling into lettuce leaves and top with peanuts, green onions, and sesame seeds. Serve!
