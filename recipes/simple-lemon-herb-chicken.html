Simple Lemon Herb Chicken

Description

This lemon-herb chicken is a simple, quick, and delicious dish. All you need are a few herbs, a lemon, and of course, the chicken! The amount of spices is completely up to you. You can add more or less according to your taste. Recipe from allrecipes.com by Carolyn Stilwell

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 2 (5 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 medium lemon, juiced, divided
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 pinch dried oregano

Garnish

  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Place chicken in a bowl; pour 1/2 of the lemon juice over chicken and season with salt.
  2. Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. Place chicken into hot oil. Add remaining lemon juice and oregano; season with black pepper. Cook chicken until golden brown and the juices run clear, 5-10 minutes per side.
  3. Garnish chicken with parsley to serve.
